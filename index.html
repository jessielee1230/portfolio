Jessicorn's Wonderland

Creativity is a wild mind and a disciplined eye.

  • Theatre for You

    'Theatre For You' was designed to create a playful and engaging interactive space at the Footbridge Gallery on the campus of the University of Sydney. It utilises a Kinect camera sensor to capture users‘ contours and presents them in an artistic silhouette of colourful particles, thereby incorporating the users as part of the artwork.

  • WonderSmile

    This is a two week web project for Australian Brand Invisable Aligner Brand - Wonder Smile.

  • O'Cheer

    O'Cheer is an Australian non-alcoholic sparkling grape juice. This is a freelancing website project built on WordPress.

  • HPV Back & Forth

    This is the website introducing HPV vaccine. HPV–2 was officially introduced to China at the end of 2017. To answer people’s questions about this unfamiliar vaccine in China, I designed and coded this website for the popularization of HPV, HPV vaccine, and its connection with cervical cancer.

  • Impact Zone

    This is a website introducing the IMPACT font. Here you can get an overall understanding of IMPACT, the web safe font, including its history and ways of use.
